Leafers

Leafers seed

Leafers seed is a two-eyed creature with green skin.

Leafers seedling

Leafers seed
evolves into Leafers seedling at the age of two. It has a new stem at this stage.

Leafers sapling

The Leafers seedling evolves into a Leafers sapling, therfore, it changes its seed into a body and its stem into hair.

Leafers tree

The Leafers sapling evolves into a Leafers tree. Its nose becomes pointier and his hair becomes a mane.

Leafers ultimate

Leafers tree evolves into a Leafers ultimate. His or her grow arms, legs, a larger tail and a bigger mane.

Piceratops

Piceratops seed

Piceratops seed is a two-eyed creature with red skin.

Piceratops seedling

Piceratops seed evolves into Piceratops seedling. at the age of three. It has a new stem at this stage.

Piceratops sapling

Piceratops seedling evolves into Piceratops sapling at the age of seven. It has a wing, a body and hair.

Piceratops tree

Piceratops seedling evolves
into Piceratops tree Piceratops tree has a neck, tail and feet.

Piceratops Ultimate

This is the last point of its evolution. The dragon now has a fierce face, a bigger tail and a longer tail.
